In-room dining lets you retreat to the seclusion and relaxation of your guest room for the night with your traveling companion. You decide just how and when you’d like your meal served. Don’t be surprised if you work up an appetite by just reading the mouthwatering menu. The menu is also available in your room by turning on the television and looking at the on-screen guest directory.
