Rated in the top 10 of all public and resort courses across Canada by The Globe and Mail, the RidgeRock Course is just spectacular.

Winding through the forested hilltops, it shows off incredible views of the valley below, offering a unique combination of challenges and enjoyment.  Our 8000 square foot RidgeRock Course Clubhouse boasts a fully equipped pro shop along with a Cafe.  Our renowned Golf Academy is located at the RidgeRock Course with instruction for all ages and skill levels.  Check out our 10-acre driving range to practice before a game or just to hit a bucket of balls.
