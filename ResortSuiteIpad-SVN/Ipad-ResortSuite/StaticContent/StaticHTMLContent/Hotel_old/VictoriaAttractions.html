Victoria Attractions

Butchart Gardens

The Butchart Gardens, considered one of the most spectacular gardens in the world,  is located just a five minutes from the Lodge. Open year round, the Gardens continually change to reflect the seasons. With 55 acres of impeccably designed and maintained gardens it is easy to spend a whole day or more simply wandering.

The Butchart Gardens also offers outstanding entertainment, in addition to the plentitude of floral gardens. In the summer an exciting fireworks display occurs every Saturday night.

Visit http://www.butchartgardens.com for more information on the gardens and entertainment schedules. 
We also offer a relaxing daily water shuttle to the gardens.

Royal BC Museum

British Columbia's past, present and future are alive at the Royal BC Museum. With a collection of more than seven million unique objects and documents, there's something incredible to see every time you visit.The RBCM is located in downtown Victoria, on the Inner Harbour.

 

Averill Creek Vineyard

 

The Averill Creek Vineyard is the result of one man's 30 year passion for wine. Over the past decade, physician Andy Johnston has been preparing for a dramatic career change by apprenticing in the vineyards of Italy, France, Australia, and New Zealand. Averill Creek is the vineyard of his dreams. Today, Averill Creek's wines are among the most popular wines on the island.
