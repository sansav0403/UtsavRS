Enter this spacious parlor through the double-door, step-down entry. The suite features fine furnishings such as an L-shaped sectional, table and four chairs and a work desk. There’s a wet bar set up for bartending service, and a modern floating stone gas fireplace accents the central gathering area. The custom media center features backlit transparent shelving, 42” HDTV and art objects. Nearby, a matching custom-designed cabinet cleverly camouflages a Murphy bed. This suite is decorated in the WAC’s modernist look, featuring espresso-stained furnishings as well as bold colors on the walls and in the fine fabrics. A beautifully designed bathroom is discreetly tucked away inside the main entrance foyer. Select sketches by Dale Chihuly grace the walls of this suite. Sliding glass doors open onto petite balconies replete with plantings. The room can expand to either a one- or two-bedroom suite if the adjoining Premier Guest Rooms are reserved. The President’s Suite Parlor was designed expressly for members who wish to entertain in a gracious, impressive space.

Size: 975 square feet
Bedding type: Queen Murphy bed
